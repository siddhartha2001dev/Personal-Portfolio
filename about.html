Who I Am

I am an enthusiastic Electronics and Communication Engineering undergraduate at Techno India University, with a diploma in Electrical Engineering from Technique Polytechnic Institute. My academic path has given me a robust foundation in core engineering concepts and a practical, problem-solving mindset.

Over time I've developed a deep interest in software development and web technologies — particularly front-end development. I possess a strong command of HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and frameworks like Bootstrap and Tailwind CSS. My expertise lies in crafting responsive, user-friendly, and aesthetically pleasing interfaces that focus on performance, accessibility, and great user experience.

Beyond tech, I'm proficient in C, C++ and Python, with a foundational understanding of Data Structures and Algorithms. I'm currently seeking challenging opportunities in front-end development, UI/UX design, and software engineering where I can contribute, keep growing, and thrive.
